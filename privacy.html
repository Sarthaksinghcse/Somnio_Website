1. Introduction

Welcome to Somnio. We are committed to protecting your privacy while helping you quiet your mind and rebuild your relationship with sleep. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, secure, and handle your information when you use the Somnio iOS application and our website.

We believe that your personal health data is yours alone. Somnio is designed around a privacy-first model, emphasizing on-device processing to minimize the collection of personal information and safeguard your sleep architecture.

2. Data We Collect

To provide you with personalized Cognitive Behavioral Therapy for Insomnia (CBT-I) dashboards and insights, Somnio accesses the following categories of information:

Apple HealthKit Data

With your explicit permission, Somnio reads data directly from Apple's HealthKit framework database on your iOS device. This includes:

  • Sleep Architecture: Sleep stages (REM, Core, Deep, Awake), sleep duration, and sleep latency.
  • Cardiovascular Health: Heart rate, Resting Heart Rate (RHR), and Heart Rate Variability (HRV) during sleep.
  • Respiratory Metrics: Respiratory rate and blood oxygen saturation (SpO₂).

User-Entered Data

We collect informational inputs that you manually provide within the app to support CBT-I exercises, including:

  • Bedtime sleep journals and cognitive thought logs.
  • Sleep efficiency answers and subjective sleep quality ratings.
  • Bedtime delay logs and wakefulness targets.

3. How We Use Data

Somnio uses the data collected from your Apple Watch and manual entries strictly to drive personalized sleep coaching features:

  • Calculating daily Sleep Efficiency scores to guide bedtime scheduling.
  • Rendering historical analytics, charts, and sleep architecture progress.
  • Powering interactive CBT-I coaching components and cognitive reframing schedules.
  • Delivering tailored bedtime recommendations and relaxation breathing guides.

Strict HealthKit Restrictions: In compliance with Apple Developer Guidelines, we do not use HealthKit data for marketing, advertising, or profiling purposes. Your health metrics are used solely to deliver the core features of the Somnio app.

4. Storage & Security

Your privacy is hardcoded into our architecture:

  • Local Database: By default, your health metrics and sleep logs are processed locally on your device inside iOS's secure application sandbox.
  • HealthKit Security: HealthKit data is encrypted by the iOS operating system when your device is locked.
  • Supabase Cloud Synchronization (Optional): If you choose to enable cloud backups, your data is transferred using end-to-end Transport Layer Security (TLS 1.3) to our secure database hosted on Supabase, which is encrypted at rest using AES-256.

5. Sharing & Third-Party Transfers

We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal health data to third parties. We will only share data in the following restricted scenarios:

  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, regulation, or legally binding government subpoenas.
  • Service Providers: Secure platform partners (such as Supabase for database backups or HealthKit system daemons) strictly to sustain operational services under confidentiality terms.

6. Your Controls & Permissions

You have full sovereignty over your data at all times:

  • HealthKit Access: You can grant, modify, or revoke Somnio's access to your Apple Health data at any time via the Settings app on iOS (Settings > Privacy > Health > Somnio).
  • Data Deletion: You can delete your entire local application database and sleep logs directly from the settings menu inside the Somnio app. If you have enabled cloud sync, deleting your account will permanently purge all cloud backups.
