See your sleep

Somnio gets data from your Apple Watch. Every morning, check your sleep score, total sleep, and sleep stages. It also tracks how long it takes you to fall asleep.

Clinically-Proven CBT-I Techniques

Somnio automatically tracks your sleep onset delay. If you take more than 20 minutes to fall asleep, Somnio applies the core CBT-I stimulus control rule, guiding you out of bed to break the link between wakefulness and your sleeping space.

Weekly reports for your doctor

Every week, Somnio makes a report of your sleep score and stages. You can view this report anytime and share the PDF with your doctor.

Ela — Quiet Your Racing Mind

Bedtime anxiety and somatic arousal keep your brain on high alert. Ela acts as a digital thought-dumping diary. Speak your worries freely before bed to process them, reduce cognitive arousal, and clear a path for sleep.

Relax and wind down

The Relaxation tab helps you calm down. Follow the simple breathing guide or listen to sleep music like rainfall and piano. This helps you fall asleep faster.
